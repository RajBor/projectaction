"Brief me on TOPCon vs PERC adoption in India for FY26"
Adoption shift summary
TOPCon now accounts for ~58% of new module orders in India for FY26 (vs ~32% in FY24). Premier, Waaree and Vikram have already retooled. PERC capacity additions have effectively stopped after Q1 FY26.
Strategic implication
Acquirers should index TOPCon-ready capacity into target valuations — PERC-only fleets carry a 2-3 year retooling drag and a 12-15% efficiency gap.
